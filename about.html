About Smart Digital Food Menu

This project helps people with health conditions choose safe foods when eating at restaurants. Designed for awareness in Bangladesh, it filters food choices based on medical restrictions.

Our Mission

At Smart Digital Food Menu, we believe that everyone deserves to enjoy dining out, regardless of their health conditions. Our mission is to make restaurant dining safer and more enjoyable for people with dietary restrictions by providing clear, accessible information about food options.

How It Works

Simply select your health condition(s) from our list, and our system will instantly filter restaurant menu items to show you which foods are safe to eat and which ones you should avoid. Our recommendations are based on medical guidelines and nutritional information.

Who We Help

Our service is designed for people with various health conditions including diabetes, celiac disease, hypertension, chronic kidney disease, lactose intolerance, gout, and irritable bowel syndrome (IBS). We're continuously expanding our database to include more health conditions and food options.
